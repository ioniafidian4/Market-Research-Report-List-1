<p><p>The timber wrap films market encompasses various types, including HDPE, LDPE, and PP wrap films. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) wrap films offer high strength and durability, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) wrap films are more flexible and provide excellent clarity, ideal for protective packaging. PP (Polypropylene) wrap films are known for their resistance to chemicals and moisture, making them versatile for various timber preservation needs. Each type serves distinct roles in enhancing the protection and transport of timber products.</p></p>
