<p><p>The Agricultural Telemetry System market consists of two primary types: Single-Channel Telemetry and Multi-Channel Telemetry. Single-Channel Telemetry focuses on transmitting data from one sensor or device, making it simpler and cost-effective for limited applications. In contrast, Multi-Channel Telemetry allows for simultaneous data transmission from multiple sensors, providing a comprehensive view of agricultural conditions. This capability enhances data collection and monitoring, enabling better decision-making in resource management, crop monitoring, and overall agricultural productivity.</p></p>
