<p><p>The performance coating market is categorized into several types, including water-based, solvent-based, and others. Water-based coatings utilize water as a primary solvent, offering environmental benefits and low VOC emissions, making them popular in eco-conscious applications. Solvent-based coatings, on the other hand, use organic solvents and are known for their durability and faster drying times, making them suitable for industrial uses. The "others" category encompasses specialty coatings like powder coatings and UV-curable coatings, catering to niche markets with specific performance requirements.</p></p>
