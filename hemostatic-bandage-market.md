<p><p>The hemostatic bandage market can be categorized into two main types: ordinary bandages and adhesive bandages. Ordinary bandages are primarily used for general wound dressing and do not possess specific hemostatic properties. In contrast, adhesive bandages are designed to adhere to the skin, providing a seal that promotes clotting and reduces bleeding. Both types aim to facilitate wound healing, but adhesive bandages offer enhanced convenience and effectiveness in controlling hemorrhage, making them a vital option in medical and emergency settings.</p></p>
