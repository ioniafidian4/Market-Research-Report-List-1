<p><p>The Power Over Ethernet (PoE) market includes two main types of Power Sourcing Equipment (PSE): PSE controllers and PSE integrated circuits (ICs). PSE controllers manage power distribution across connected devices, ensuring optimal performance and efficiency in powering network equipment like IP cameras and phones. PSE ICs, on the other hand, are specialized semiconductor components that facilitate power delivery and data transmission through Ethernet cables. Together, they enable seamless integration of power and data, supporting the growing demand for connected devices.</p></p>
