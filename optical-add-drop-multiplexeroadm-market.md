<p><p>The Optical Add-Drop Multiplexer (OADM) market is primarily divided into two types: Fixed Optical Add-Drop Multiplexers and Reconfigurable Optical Add-Drop Multiplexers. Fixed OADMs are designed for specific network configurations, allowing the addition or removal of predetermined wavelengths without flexibility. In contrast, Reconfigurable OADMs offer dynamic wavelength management, enabling the adjustment of dropped and added channels as network demands change. This adaptability supports efficient bandwidth utilization and enhances network performance, making reconfigurable units increasingly favored in modern telecommunication infrastructures.</p></p>
