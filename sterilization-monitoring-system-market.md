<p><p>The Sterilization Monitoring System market comprises three key types: </p><p>1. **Biological Monitoring System** involves using viable bacterial spores to assess the effectiveness of sterilization processes, ensuring that conditions were sufficient to eliminate pathogens.</p><p>   </p><p>2. **Chemical Monitoring System** utilizes indicator strips or devices that change color in response to certain sterilization processes, providing immediate visual confirmation of exposure.</p><p>3. **Mechanical Monitoring System** focuses on tracking the physical parameters of sterilization cycles, such as temperature and pressure, to ensure compliance with established protocols. Each system plays a crucial role in ensuring safety and efficacy in sterilization practices.</p></p>
