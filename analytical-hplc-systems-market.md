<p><strong>The Analytical HPLC Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Normal Phase HPLC</li><li>Reverse Phase HPLC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Analytical HPLC systems encompass two main types: normal phase HPLC and reverse phase HPLC. Normal phase HPLC utilizes a polar stationary phase and a non-polar mobile phase, primarily separating compounds by their polarity, making it effective for analyzing non-polar substances. In contrast, reverse phase HPLC employs a non-polar stationary phase and a polar mobile phase, favoring the separation of polar compounds. Together, these methodologies cater to diverse applications in pharmaceuticals, environmental testing, and food analysis, facilitating precise compound characterization.</p></p>
