<p><p>Online invoice software is categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based invoice software operates over the internet, allowing users to access their invoicing from any location with internet access. This model often features subscription pricing and automatic updates. Conversely, on-premise software is installed directly on a user's computer or server, offering more control over data and customization, but requiring upfront costs and maintenance. Each type caters to different business needs and preferences.</p></p>
