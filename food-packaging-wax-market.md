Natural wax includes substances like beeswax and carnauba wax, known for their biodegradable properties and excellent barrier qualities, making them suitable for organic and sustainable packaging solutions. Mineral wax, derived from petroleum, offers cost-effectiveness and durability, commonly used for moisture-proof packaging. Both types serve to enhance shelf life, maintain product quality, and provide a protective layer for various food products in the market.</p></p>
