<p><strong>The Residential Combi Boilers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-condensing Boilers</li><li>Condensing Boilers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The residential combi boilers market includes two main types: non-condensing and condensing boilers. Non-condensing boilers operate at higher temperatures and do not utilize waste heat, making them less efficient and more suitable for older systems. In contrast, condensing boilers capture and reuse heat from exhaust gases, significantly improving energy efficiency and reducing emissions. As energy standards become stricter, the demand for condensing boilers is increasing, while non-condensing models are gradually being phased out in favor of greener alternatives.</p></p>
