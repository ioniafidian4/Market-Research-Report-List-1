<p><strong>The Steering Tie Rod Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inner Tie Rod</li><li>Outer Tie Rod</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steering tie rod market is primarily segmented into two types: inner tie rods and outer tie rods. Inner tie rods connect the steering rack to the outer tie rods, playing a critical role in steering precision and vehicle handling. Outer tie rods, on the other hand, link the inner tie rods to the steering knuckles, facilitating the vehicle's turning capabilities. Both components are essential for the overall functioning of a vehicle's steering system, ensuring safety and performance on the road.</p></p>
