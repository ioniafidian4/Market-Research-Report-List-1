<p><ul><li>Membrane Plasma Separation Technique</li><li>Centrifugation</li><li>Filtration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plasma component separator market encompasses various technologies for isolating blood plasma from other components. The membrane plasma separation technique utilizes selective membranes to separate plasma based on size and permeability, ensuring efficient component isolation. Centrifugation employs rapid spinning to separate blood components based on density, effectively collecting plasma. Meanwhile, filtration uses filter media to separate plasma from cells and other particles, relying on size exclusion principles. Together, these methods cater to diverse medical and industrial applications in plasma processing.</p></p>
