<p><p>Methoxy Polyethylene Glycol (MPEG) is categorized into three main market types based on its physical form: transparent liquid, white or yellowish paste, and white flakes. The transparent liquid form is commonly used for applications requiring high solubility and fluidity, ideal for formulations in personal care and pharmaceuticals. The white or yellowish paste is often utilized in industrial applications, providing a thicker consistency suitable for various formulations. White flakes offer a solid form, benefiting storage and transportation while allowing easy integration into diverse products.</p></p>
