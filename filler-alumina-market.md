<p><ul><li>Micron Grade</li><li>Millimeter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The filler alumina market can be segmented into two main types: micron grade and millimeter grade. Micron grade alumina consists of finely processed particles, typically ranging from 1 to 100 micrometers, making it suitable for applications requiring high purity and a smooth finish, such as in coatings and plastics. Millimeter grade alumina, on the other hand, consists of larger particles, usually over 1 millimeter, which are often used in construction, ceramics, and abrasive materials for bulk applications.</p></p>
