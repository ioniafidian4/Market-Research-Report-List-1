<p><p>The stadium seating market is divided into two primary types: foldable and non-foldable seating. Foldable seating is designed for versatility, allowing seats to be collapsed and stored away when not in use, making it ideal for multipurpose venues. Non-foldable seating, on the other hand, provides a fixed arrangement, offering stability and comfort for permanent installations like sports arenas or concert halls. Both types cater to different requirements based on space efficiency and the intended usage of the venue.</p></p>
