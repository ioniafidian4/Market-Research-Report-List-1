<p><p>The stainless pipe market is primarily categorized into two types: welded and seamless pipes. Welded pipes are produced by joining metal sheets or strips through welding methods, providing good structural integrity and making them suitable for various applications. Seamless pipes, on the other hand, are manufactured without any joints, offering higher strength and resistance to pressure, making them ideal for high-stress environments. Both types cater to different industry needs, influencing their demand and market dynamics.</p></p>
