<p><p>The Positive Temperature Coefficient (PTC) temperature sensor market includes two main types: CPTC (Continuous Positive Temperature Coefficient) sensors and PPTC (Polymeric Positive Temperature Coefficient) sensors. CPTC sensors exhibit a consistent increase in resistance with rising temperature, allowing for precise temperature measurement. In contrast, PPTC sensors are primarily used for overcurrent protection and automatically reset after cooling down. Both types are utilized in various applications, including automotive, industrial, and consumer electronics, due to their reliability and sensitivity to temperature changes.</p></p>
