<p><p>The Aviation Active Noise and Vibration Control System market comprises two primary types: Active Noise Control Systems (ANCS) and Active Vibration Control Systems (AVCS). ANCS is designed to reduce unwanted ambient noise in aircraft cabins, enhancing passenger comfort by using sound-cancellation technology. In contrast, AVCS focuses on minimizing vibrations in aircraft structures, improving stability and reducing wear on components. Both systems are integral for optimizing performance, safety, and overall passenger experience in modern aviation.</p></p>
