<p><ul><li>Deformed Steel</li><li>Mild Steel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Green Rebar market is primarily categorized into two types: deformed steel rebar and mild steel rebar. Deformed steel rebar, characterized by its surface ridges, enhances mechanical bonding with concrete, making it ideal for high-strength structures. In contrast, mild steel rebar, with its smooth surface, offers flexibility and is typically used in less demanding applications. Both types are essential for sustainable construction practices, ensuring durability while reducing environmental impact through the use of recycled materials in production.</p></p>
