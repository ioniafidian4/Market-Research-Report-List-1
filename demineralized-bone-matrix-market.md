<p><p>Demineralized Bone Matrix (DBM) is a specialized bone graft material derived from human or animal bones, processed to remove mineral content while preserving the organic matrix, primarily collagen proteins. DBM is widely used in various orthopedic and dental surgical procedures to promote bone healing and regeneration. It serves as a scaffold for new bone growth, making it a popular choice in transplant surgeries and for treating bone defects.</p><p>The Demineralized Bone Matrix Market is witnessing steady growth, projected to expand at a CAGR of 1.25% during the forecast period. <p><p>The Demineralized Bone Matrix (DBM) market comprises three primary types: gel, putty, and putty with chips. DBM gel is a liquid formulation that provides a highly injectable option, promoting ease of application in surgical procedures. DBM putty is a semi-solid option that offers excellent moldability and stability, making it suitable for various orthopedic applications. DBM putty with chips combines the benefits of putty with small bone chips, enhancing structural support while facilitating bone healing in complex surgical scenarios.</p></p>
