<p><p>Stainless steel pressure gauges are categorized based on their mounting types: lower mount, back mount, and panel mount. Lower mount gauges feature a bottom connection for easy installation on pipes or tanks, making them suitable for compact spaces. Back mount gauges have a rear connection, allowing for flush mounting against surfaces, ideal for control panels. Panel mount gauges are designed to be integrated into control panels or dashboards, providing visible pressure readings while ensuring durability and resistance to harsh environments.</p></p>
