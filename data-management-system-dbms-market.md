<p><p>A Data Management System (DBMS) is software that enables the creation, management, and manipulation of databases. The market can be broadly divided into relational and non-relational segments. Relational DBMS use structured query language (SQL) and store data in tables, ensuring data integrity and support for complex queries. In contrast, non-relational DBMS, often referred to as NoSQL, handle unstructured or semi-structured data, providing flexibility, scalability, and performance for diverse data types and applications, such as big data and real-time analytics.</p></p>
