<p><strong>The Nitrate Ion Selective Electrodes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Liquid Membrane</li><li>PVC Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nitrate ion selective electrode (ISE) market is mainly categorized into two types: liquid membrane and PVC membrane electrodes. Liquid membrane electrodes utilize a solution containing a selective ionophore that responds specifically to nitrate ions, allowing for high sensitivity and accuracy. In contrast, PVC membrane electrodes are constructed using a polyvinyl chloride matrix combined with an ion-selective additive, providing a stable and robust platform for nitrate detection. Both types are integral in various applications, including environmental monitoring and agricultural testing.</p></p>
