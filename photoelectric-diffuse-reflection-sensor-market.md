<p><strong>The Photoelectric Diffuse reflection Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PNP</li><li>NPN</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photoelectric diffuse reflection sensor market is primarily categorized into two types: PNP and NPN sensors. PNP sensors provide a positive voltage output when activated, allowing them to sink current to the load. In contrast, NPN sensors output a negative voltage, sourcing current away from the load. These types differ in their wiring configurations and applications; PNP sensors are often used in high-side switching, while NPN sensors are suited for low-side applications, influencing market trends and user preferences.</p></p>
