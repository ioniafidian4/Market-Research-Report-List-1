<p><p>The Glass Vaccine Vial market primarily consists of two types: Molded Glass Vials and Tubular Glass Vials. Molded Glass Vials are produced by shaping molten glass into specific forms, allowing for varied designs and sizes, often used for large-volume vaccines. Tubular Glass Vials, on the other hand, are made from pre-formed glass tubes and are typically used for smaller volumes, providing uniformity and efficient production. Both types offer excellent barrier properties, ensuring vaccine stability and safety.</p></p>
