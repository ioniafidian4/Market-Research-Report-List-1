<p><strong>The Head Mounted Display (HMD) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Slide-on HMD</li><li>Discrete HMD</li><li>Integrated HMD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Head Mounted Displays (HMDs) are wearable devices that provide immersive visual experiences. The market can be categorized into three types: </p><p>1. **Slide-on HMD**: These attach to existing eyewear, offering a less intrusive option for users. </p><p>2. **Discrete HMD**: Compact and lightweight, they blend seamlessly into everyday use, often resembling traditional eyewear. </p><p>3. **Integrated HMD**: These are standalone devices with built-in displays and processing capabilities, designed for dedicated applications like gaming or virtual training, providing a fully immersive experience.</p></p>
