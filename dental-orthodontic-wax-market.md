<p><p>The dental orthodontic wax market includes three primary types: beeswax, paraffin wax, and carnauba wax. Beeswax, derived from honeybees, offers a natural and pliable option for discomfort relief from braces. Paraffin wax, a petroleum-based product, is widely used for its smooth texture and effectiveness in creating a barrier against irritation. Carnauba wax, sourced from the leaves of a Brazilian palm tree, provides a harder, more durable solution, often used in professional settings. Each type serves to enhance patient comfort throughout orthodontic treatment.</p></p>
