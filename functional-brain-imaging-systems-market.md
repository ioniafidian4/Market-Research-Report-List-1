<p><p>The functional brain imaging systems market includes several key technologies. Functional MRI (fMRI) systems measure brain activity by detecting changes in blood flow, providing detailed images of brain function. Functional near-infrared spectroscopy (fNIRS) systems use light to monitor oxygen levels in the brain, offering a portable alternative. Positron emission tomography (PET) systems visualize metabolic processes by tracking radioactive tracers. Other market types encompass emerging imaging techniques and hybrid systems that integrate different modalities for enhanced brain activity analysis.</p></p>
