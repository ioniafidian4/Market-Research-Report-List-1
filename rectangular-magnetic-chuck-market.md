<p><ul><li>Electro-Permanent Chuck</li><li>Permanent Magnet Chuck</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rectangular magnetic chucks are essential in machining for securely holding workpieces. They primarily include electro-permanent chucks and permanent magnet chucks. Electro-permanent chucks use electrical inputs to switch between magnetized and demagnetized states, offering flexibility and power efficiency. In contrast, permanent magnet chucks deploy fixed magnets for consistent holding force without needing electricity, ensuring reliable performance in various machining processes. Both types enhance productivity by providing stable and precise workpiece positioning in CNC and grinding applications.</p></p>
