<p><strong>The Automotive Windshield Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laminated Glass</li><li>Tempered Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive windshield market comprises two primary types: laminated glass and tempered glass. Laminated glass consists of two layers of glass bonded with a polymer interlayer, providing safety by preventing shattering and offering enhanced sound insulation. It is commonly used in passenger vehicles. Tempered glass, on the other hand, is heat-treated to increase strength and shatter into small, less harmful pieces upon impact, commonly used for rear and side windows. Both serve crucial safety and performance roles in modern vehicles.</p></p>
