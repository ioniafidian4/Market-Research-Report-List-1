<p><p>The prosthetic heart valve market includes various types designed to replace damaged heart valves. Transcatheter heart valves are minimally invasive options delivered via catheter, suitable for patients at high surgical risk. Tissue heart valves, made from biological materials, mimic natural valves and have a lifespan of 10-20 years. Mechanical heart valves are durable devices made from synthetic materials, providing long-term functionality but requiring lifelong anticoagulation therapy. These market segments cater to diverse patient needs and surgical approaches in cardiac care.</p></p>
