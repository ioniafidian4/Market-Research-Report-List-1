<p><p>The automotive relays market is segmented into closed type and open type relays. Closed type relays feature a sealed design that protects internal components from environmental factors, making them suitable for harsh conditions in vehicles. In contrast, open type relays have exposed components, allowing for easier maintenance and cooler operation but are more vulnerable to damage. Both types serve essential roles in automotive electrical systems, controlling circuits and ensuring reliable operation of various vehicle functions.</p></p>
