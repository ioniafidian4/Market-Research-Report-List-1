<p><p>The stretch wrappers market is divided into two main types: semi-automatic and automatic stretch wrappers. Semi-automatic stretch wrappers require manual loading of products onto the wrapping machine, with the wrapping process automated afterward, making them suitable for low to medium production volumes. In contrast, automatic stretch wrappers fully automate the wrapping process, integrating with conveyor systems and requiring minimal human intervention. This type is ideal for high-volume operations, enhancing efficiency and consistency in packaging.</p></p>
