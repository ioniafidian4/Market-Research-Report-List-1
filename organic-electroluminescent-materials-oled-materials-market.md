<p><p>Organic Electroluminescent Materials (OLED materials) are essential in the production of OLED displays, which are used in televisions, smartphones, and other devices. The market is primarily divided into two types: emission layer materials, which emit light when an electric current is applied, and common layer materials, which facilitate charge transport and support the overall device structure. Emission layer materials directly influence color and brightness, while common layer materials ensure efficiency and stability, driving advancements in display technologies.</p></p>
