<p><p>The Home Wi-Fi 6 router market is primarily divided into dual-band and tri-band routers. Dual-band routers operate on both 2.4 GHz and 5 GHz frequencies, providing a balanced performance suitable for most households. In contrast, tri-band routers add an additional 5 GHz band, offering enhanced capacity and speed for multiple high-demand devices simultaneously. This makes tri-band routers ideal for larger homes or households with heavy streaming, gaming, and smart devices, as they reduce congestion and improve overall network efficiency.</p></p>
