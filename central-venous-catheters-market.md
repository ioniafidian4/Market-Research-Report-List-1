<p><p>The Central Venous Catheter (CVC) market is divided into several types based on the number of lumens. Single-lumen catheters are designed for simple intravenous access, while double-lumen catheters allow simultaneous medication delivery and blood sampling. Triple-lumen catheters provide even greater flexibility for multiple therapies and fluid administration. Other types may include unique designs for specific clinical needs, such as dialysis or long-term use. Each type serves distinct medical purposes, catering to varying patient requirements and healthcare settings.</p></p>
