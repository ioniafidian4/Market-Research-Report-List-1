<p><p>The pediatric nasal aspirator market is divided into two main types: electric nasal aspirators and manual nasal aspirators. Electric nasal aspirators utilize battery or electric power to create suction, providing an efficient and often quieter option for clearing nasal congestion in infants and young children. Manual nasal aspirators, on the other hand, rely on the caregiver's suction through a bulb or a tube, offering a more affordable and portable choice. Both types are designed to improve respiratory comfort in children.</p></p>
