<p><strong>The Single-head Hot Runner System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Heating</li><li>External Heating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The single-head hot runner system market can be divided into two primary types: internal heating and external heating. Internal heating systems feature heaters positioned within the manifold, providing direct temperature control and enhancing thermal efficiency. This design minimizes heat loss and promotes uniform melt flow. In contrast, external heating systems have heaters located outside the manifold, allowing for easier maintenance and inspection. While both types aim to improve the injection molding process, each serves different operational preferences and production requirements.</p></p>
