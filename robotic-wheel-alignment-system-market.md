<p><p>The Robotic Wheel Alignment System market primarily comprises two types: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) Wheel Alignment Systems and 3D Wheel Alignment Systems. CCD systems utilize optical technology to measure wheel angles with precision, providing reliable alignments for vehicles. In contrast, 3D systems employ advanced imaging and laser technology to deliver real-time measurements and enhanced accuracy. Both types cater to varying automotive service needs, ensuring optimal vehicle performance, improved tire longevity, and enhanced safety through precise wheel alignment.</p></p>
