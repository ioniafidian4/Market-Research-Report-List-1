<p><strong>The Aboveground Fuel Tanks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Wall</li><li>Double Wall</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aboveground fuel tanks market comprises primarily two types: single wall and double wall tanks. Single wall tanks consist of a single layer, suitable for lower risk environments, but they lack secondary containment. In contrast, double wall tanks feature an outer shell that provides secondary containment, minimizing the risk of leaks and improving safety. Double wall tanks are often preferred in more regulated applications, offering greater environmental protection, while single wall tanks are generally less expensive and easier to install.</p></p>
