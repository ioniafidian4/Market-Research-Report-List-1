<p><ul><li>Pneumatic Atomizer</li><li>Ultrasonic Atomizer</li><li>Net Atomizer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The household atomizer market is segmented into three main types: pneumatic, ultrasonic, and net atomizers. Pneumatic atomizers use compressed air to create a fine mist, suitable for larger spaces. Ultrasonic atomizers utilize high-frequency vibrations to produce a cool mist, ideal for personal use and small areas. Net atomizers employ a vibrating mesh to disperse liquid into micro-particles, ensuring efficient and quiet operation. Each type caters to different consumer needs, from comfort and efficiency to portability and aesthetic design.</p></p>
