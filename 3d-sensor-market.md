<p><ul><li>Stereo Vision</li><li>Structured Light</li><li>Time of Flight</li><li>Ultrasound</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D sensor market comprises several key types: </p><p>1. **Stereo Vision** utilizes two or more cameras to capture images from different angles, enabling depth perception through triangulation. </p><p>2. **Structured Light** projects a known light pattern onto a surface and analyzes its deformation to determine 3D shape and distance. </p><p>3. **Time of Flight (ToF)** measures the time it takes for light to travel to an object and back, allowing precise distance calculations. </p><p>4. **Ultrasound** employs sound waves to detect distances and shapes, often used in medical and industrial applications.</p></p>
